SANCTUARY CHOIR

About

The Trinity Sanctuary Choir is the adult choir functioning for our church; we have a group of approximately 200 members. We are always welcoming another one of God's children looking to praise and worship through song! Led by director Bryan Johnson, we are the largest choir of the church and always love joining our members in worshipping the Lord.
